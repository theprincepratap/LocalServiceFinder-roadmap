The Challenge We're Solving

Finding trusted local help is often a frustrating experience for both customers and skilled workers. We aim to fix that.

For Users

  • ✗ Difficulty finding reliable, verified local workers.
  • ✗ Lack of a single, trusted platform for diverse services.
  • ✗ Uncertainty about skills, availability, and fair pricing.

For Workers

  • ✗ Limited digital presence to showcase their skills.
  • ✗ Inconsistent access to job opportunities in their area.
  • ✗ Challenges in building a trusted online reputation.

Our Vision: A Unified Platform

A web-based application designed to bridge the gap between local service needs and skilled professionals.

System Architecture

A decoupled architecture ensures scalability and maintainability.

Frontend (React) → API (Django REST)
API (Django) ↔ Databases (PostgreSQL/Cassandra)
User Interaction → Payment Gateway

Secure Payment Flow

  1. 1. Book & Pay

    User pays securely via the integrated gateway.

  2. 2. Escrow Hold

    Payment is held safely by the platform.

  3. 3. Service Complete

    User confirms the service is satisfactorily completed.

  4. 4. Release Payment

    Funds are released to the worker, minus a small fee.

Technical Deep Dive

Applying computer science fundamentals to solve real-world problems.

  • ER Model & Relational Mapping: For Users, Workers, Services, and Payments.
  • Normalization: To reduce data redundancy and improve integrity.
  • PostgreSQL: For structured data and ACID-compliant transactions.
  • Indexing (B/B+ Trees): To ensure fast search queries for workers and services.
  • Cassandra: For handling scalable, semi-structured data like logs and reviews.
  • Search Algorithms: Hash maps for quick user/worker lookups.
  • Sorting Algorithms: To sort workers by rating, price, or distance.
  • Graph Algorithms (Dijkstra): For potential route optimization for workers.
  • Queues & Stacks: To manage job requests and notifications efficiently.
  • Hashing: For secure password storage and fast data retrieval.
